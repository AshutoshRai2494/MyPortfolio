About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. research scholar at the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. I joined the Ph.D. programme at IIT Mandi in August 2021 after completing my M.Tech. in Power Electronics & Drives from IIT Mandi in July 2021.

In May 2022, I was selected for the prestigious Prime Minister's Research Fellowship (PMRF) through the lateral entry channel in the 9th cycle. My research interest lies in control and analysis of power electronic converters.

Currently, I am working in the area of design and development of renewable energy integrated high power density power electronic system for charging light EVs while ensuring high power quality at grid. My research focuses on developing innovative solutions for the future of sustainable transportation.

Research Highlights

üîã Electric Vehicle Charging Solutions

The imminent global fossil fuel crisis and ever-increasing global warming have opened up opportunities for green and energy efficient electric vehicles (EV). With the prevalence of EV in automobile market the charging infrastructure also needs to grow in parity. The objective is to provide a reliable, resilient and adaptive charging solution for existing and future EVs.

‚ö° Wireless Power Transfer Technology

Widespread commercial adoption of WPT technology in EV sector globally is still insignificant as compared to conventional plug-in charging. The research focuses on developing charging stations that accommodate both plug-in and wireless charging in the same architecture for flexibility in charging of both existing plug-in EVs and future wireless EVs.

üå± Renewable Energy Integration

The research focus is to develop a high-power density power electronic system accommodating both contactless and conductive charging with integration solutions for PV source assisted through utility grid. The target is to achieve power level output of 4 kW while ensuring high-power quality at grid.
